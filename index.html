Coffee Oakla

Serving coffee with love

e.s.t 2008

Coffee Oakla has been rocking it in downtown Oakland since 2008. We wear many hats: a community coffee-house, espresso bar, best-in-class coffee gear retailer, restaurant, beer and wine bar, and event venue.

But our main mission? Bringing people together to launch what’s next for Oakla and the world. And hey, we might as well #drinkgoodcoffee while we’re at it! ☕

Here’s a quick rundown of what we offer:

  1. Coffee Roasting: We roast our coffee regularly at Pulley Collective in West Oakland.
  2. No Wi-Fi: Sorry, folks, but we’re all about real-world connections.
  3. Recognition: We’ve been selected as one of the 25 Best Coffee Shops in the Bay Area by the SF Chronicle, and the readers of the East Bay Express have voted us Best Cafe or Runner Up five times!
